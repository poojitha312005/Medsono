Medications:

  • Inhaled Corticosteroid (ICS): Fluticasone (Flovent, Arnuity)
  • Long-Acting Beta Agonist (LABA): Salmeterol (Serevent), Formoterol (Foradil)
  • Short-Acting Beta Agonist (SABA) for Rescue: Albuterol (Proventil, Ventolin), Levalbuterol (Xopenex)
  • Leukotriene Modifier: Montelukast (Singulair)
  • Other: Oral corticosteroids for severe asthma exacerbations

    Recommendations

    • Avoid triggers such as tobacco smoke, dust mites, pet dander, pollen, and air pollution.
    • Use air conditioning and air purifiers to improve indoor air quality.
    • Practice good hand hygiene to reduce the risk of respiratory infections.
    • Stay up-to-date with vaccinations, including the annual flu vaccine and pneumococcal vaccine.
