Our Departments

Medsono can help patients understand the breadth of services available and navigate to the specific care they need.

Primary Care

General medical services, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment for common illnesses like colds and flu.

Diagnosis

diagnostic services such as laboratory tests, imaging (X-rays, MRIs, CT scans), and remote monitoring devices for home testing.

Specialist Care

Medical specialties such as cardiology, neurology, psychiatry, orthopedics, etc. Each department have specialists available.

First Aid

CPR, bleeding control, and wound care, empowering individuals to make a difference in emergencies.

Telemedicine

Telemedicine enables remote healthcare delivery through digital communication technologies, allowing patients to consult with healthcare providers virtually. It provides convenient access to medical services, including consultations, diagnosis, and treatment, from the comfort of home, enhancing healthcare accessibility and efficiency.

Assisted Telemedicine

Assisted telemedicine integrates technology with personalized support, ensuring patients receive comprehensive care remotely. Our platform facilitates seamless communication between patients and healthcare providers, offering guidance and assistance throughout the telemedicine process. Experience the convenience of remote healthcare with dedicated assistance every step of the way.

Telemedicine Training

telemedicine training courses and resources for healthcare professionals. Include interactive modules, webinars, case studies, and certification programs covering telemedicine technology, best practices, regulatory compliance, and ethical considerations.
